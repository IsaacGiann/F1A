After a very dominant year from Red Bull, the rest of the table have some work to do in preparation for the 2024 season. Max Verstappen led almost every race without competition making this 2023 season a blow out! The Red showed the highest top speed without sacrificing time around corners, making the RB19 incredibly dominant. Max's teammate Sergio Perez had a great start to the year but struggled to find his footing again for the rest of the season. There was rumours about the transfer of Sergio Perez out of the Red Bull with his seat being filled with Aussie icon Daniel Ricciardo. Daniel took the place of Nyck de Vries only 10 races into his rookie season as Daniel was showing better test times around the track. Alpha Tauri being the sister team to Red Bull, was a test for Daniel to see if he could compete with Sergio on the track. The 2024 season is yet to see any driver change for Red Bull or Alpha Tauri as all four drivers remain the same as of now. However, Alpha Tauri have changed their name to Visa Cash App RB Formula One Team and Alfa Romeo have changed their name to Stake F1 Team Kick Sauber.

Max Verstappen came first place by a long shot carrying Red Bull to the 2023 season Championship. A few new changes are being made for this new upcoming season of Formula 1. New tracks are making there way back in to the calender with Japan and China joining in. Another country is providing a new track and is also having it's time in the lime light being China - Shanghai. After fans last year were heartbroken that Italy - Imola was unable to host the race due to servere weather conditions, the track is looking to redeem itself this year being placed at the end of the European summer. The 2023 season saw Imola flooded to the extent the race had to be cancelled. It's not just the Italian supporters hoping this year we get to see the magical track burn some rubber!
